Transforming India's Packaging Industry

Kraft Paperz is India's premier B2B platform connecting packaging manufacturers with premium raw materials. We serve manufacturers of packing boxes, cartons, tubes, crockery items, paper bags, plates, and corrugated rolls across the nation.

Our innovative mobile app combines traditional industry expertise with cutting-edge technology, making material procurement the easiest, fastest experience in the market.

The Growing
Packaging Industry

Packaging, once a mere container, has evolved into a sophisticated discipline, standing at the intersection of science and engineering. It's a critical component across diverse sectors, ensuring product integrity, safety, and market appeal.

Globally, the packaging industry holds the distinction of being the third largest, indispensable to the pharmaceutical, Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), and electronics industries, among others.
